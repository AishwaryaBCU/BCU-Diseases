Chronic Kidney Disease Prediction

Predictor that You can Trust

Chronic Kidney Disease (CKD) is a condition characterized by the gradual loss of kidney function over time, which can lead to kidney failure if not managed properly. Early detection and treatment are crucial to slowing the progression of the disease.

Machine Learning Project

This CKD prediction tool is developed using machine learning techniques to predict the likelihood of chronic kidney disease in individuals based on various health parameters. The model is trained on a dataset that includes parameters such as:

  • Age
  • Red Blood Cells
  • Blood Urea
  • Packed Cell Volume
  • Coronary Artery Disease
  • Blood Pressure
  • Pus Cell
  • Serum Creatinine
  • White Blood Cell Count
  • Appetite
  • Specific Gravity
  • Pus Cell Clumps
  • Sodium
  • Red Blood Cell Count
  • Pedal Edema
  • Albumin
  • Bacteria
  • Potassium
  • Hypertension
  • Anemia
  • Sugar
  • Blood Glucose Random
  • Hemoglobin
  • Diabetes Mellitus

Model Accuracy

The machine learning model used in this project achieves an accuracy of approximately 94% on the test dataset. This means that the model is able to correctly predict chronic kidney disease 94% of the time based on the given health parameters.
