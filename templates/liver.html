Liver Disease Prediction

Predictor that You can Trust

Liver Disease is a condition characterized by the dysfunction or damage to the liver, resulting in impaired liver function. Early detection and intervention are crucial to prevent further liver damage and complications.

Machine Learning Project

This liver disease prediction tool is developed using machine learning techniques to predict the likelihood of liver disease in individuals based on various health parameters. The model is trained on a dataset that includes parameters such as:

  • Age
  • Alkaline Phosphotase
  • Albumin
  • Gender
  • Alamine Aminotransferase
  • Albumin and Globulin Ratio
  • Total Bilirubin
  • Aspartate Aminotransferase
  • Direct Bilirubin
  • Total Proteins
